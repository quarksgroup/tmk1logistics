About us

We at TMK1 Logistics are passionate about providing end-to-end logistics solutions; an established name in freight forwarding, goods handling and customs clearance, we have our own branch offices in the Democratic Republic of Congo(Goma, Bukavu and Kinshasa) and our Head office in Rwanda(Gikondo MAGERWA, Sulfo Building 1st Floor).

We are recognized as one of the industries most qualified and knowledgeable logistics firms. Our company possesses a professional, experienced & dedicated team that handles air, sea and in-land freight Shipments from all around the World. We pride ourselves on being a solutions oriented company, and are happy to tailor a solution to meet your needs. “There are no limits to how we can facilitate you”.
